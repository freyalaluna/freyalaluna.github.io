About Me

Hi! My name is Freya LaLuna. I'm a newly-graduated developer looking to get my foot in the door within the tech industry. I completed my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science with a 3.85 GPA at Colorado State University on May 13th, 2023. I have a deep interest in backend development, database management, and distributed systems, and I'm always trying to deepen my skillset as a developer. I also have a deep passion for diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI), and I'd love to help make the tech industry a more approachable place for people from all walks of life.

Academic Projects

Here is a showcase of some of the more noteworthy projects that I collaborated on during my university career. The code for all of these, as well as many of the smaller coding assignments I've done for my classes, can be found on my Github.

Asynchronous Chess Web App

An app designed to play chess remotely and asynchronously, removing the need for both players to be present at the same time for a match to take place.

Company Project Manager

A website for managing a company's projects. A user can add workers to projects based on their qualifications and workloads, declare the completion status of projects based on the number of qualifications fulfilled, and add new projects, workers, and qualifications to the database as needed.

Circuit Learning Tool

Created as part of a research project to determine the efficacy of different annotation types in a gamefied learning environment. This tool walks participants through the creations of several basic circuits, with two different types of annotations available for guidance.
